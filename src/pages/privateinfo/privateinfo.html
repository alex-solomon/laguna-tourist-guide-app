Splash Island, is located at Southwood Ecocentrum in Biñan, Laguna, is known as the largest waterpark in the Philippines. Splash Island sits on a vast piece of real estate where more than 12 water attraction can be found. The park is so vast it can accommodate 6,000 guest at any given time.

Patterned after the famous water theme park in th United States, Splash Island has a myriad of slides, pools and other water attraction for guest of all ages. Add to these the assurance of security, the management has set up height and capability restriction for some attraction.
